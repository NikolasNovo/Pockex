Account and Data Deletion – Pockex

Pockex does not require users to create an account to use the app.

The app offers an optional cloud backup feature that uses the user’s Google Account on Android.

No in-app account

Pockex does not create or manage user accounts on its own servers. We do not store email addresses, usernames, or passwords.

How to delete your data

  • Local app data can be deleted by uninstalling the app from the device.
  • Cloud backup data can be managed and deleted by the user through their Google Account settings.

Data retention

Pockex does not retain personal data on its own servers. Any cloud data retention is governed by Google’s policies.
