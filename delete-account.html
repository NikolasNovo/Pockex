Account and Data Deletion – Pockex

Users of the Pockex app can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to info.pockex@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address used in the app

Data that will be deleted

  • User account (email, name)
  • Authentication credentials
  • App-related preferences

Retention period

All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days of the request, unless retention is required by law.
