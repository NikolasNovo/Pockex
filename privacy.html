Privacy Policy – Pockex

Pockex is a local point-of-sale (POS) application for currency exchange. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

Pockex does not require users to create an account to use the app. The app does not collect or store personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

Optional Cloud Backup

Pockex offers an optional cloud backup feature that allows users to synchronize their data across devices. This feature uses the user’s Google Account on Android or Apple ID on iOS.

Pockex does not receive or store the user’s email address, name, or authentication credentials. All cloud data management, including access and deletion, is handled by Google or Apple according to their privacy policies.

Subscriptions and Payments

Subscriptions and payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing or Apple App Store. Pockex does not have access to users’ payment information.

Advertising

Pockex may display advertisements. Advertising partners may use device identifiers as permitted by the platform to provide relevant ads.

Data Security

All data transmitted by the app is encrypted in transit using secure communication protocols (HTTPS).

Data Retention and Deletion

Pockex does not store personal data on its own servers. Locally stored data can be removed by uninstalling the app. Cloud backup data can be managed and deleted by the user through their Google Account or Apple ID settings.
